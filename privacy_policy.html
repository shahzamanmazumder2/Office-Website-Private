Privacy Policy

How We Protect and Respect Your Data

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

1 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Cloud Connect AI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website, services, and products (collectively, the "Services"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Services. This may include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and payment details when you register, make a purchase, or contact us.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can manage them through your browser settings.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide, operate, maintain, and improve our Services, personalize your experience, communicate with you, process transactions, and develop new features.

4 How We Obtain and Share Data

Cloud Connect AI does not share any user data collected directly from you with any third parties. Our core principle is maintaining the utmost privacy and confidentiality of your information.

The data and insights provided through our services are primarily derived from publicly available Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) resources. We leverage both free and paid OSINT tools to gather information for cybersecurity intelligence, connectivity analysis, and AI-driven insights. Any data utilized in our services is acquired through these legitimate channels.

We may disclose information only if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5 Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

8 Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Questions About Our Privacy Practices?

We are committed to transparency. Reach out to us anytime.
