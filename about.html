Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often.

What kind of projects does Cloud Connect AI take on?

We specialize in full-stack digital solutions including:

  • Web & mobile app development (React, Flutter, Kotlin, Swift)
  • Backend & database architecture (Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, MongoDB)
  • Cloud infrastructure & DevOps (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • AI/ML solutions, generative AI, autonomous agents & RAG systems
  • Complete digital transformation & enterprise modernization

From startups to large enterprises — we adapt to your scale and industry.

How long does it usually take to build an app or AI solution?
  • Simple MVP mobile/web app → 2–4 months
  • Medium complexity product → 4–7 months
  • Enterprise system or advanced AI solution → 6–14 months
  • AI proof-of-concept / prototype → 4–12 weeks

We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes — flexible post-launch support includes:

  • Maintenance & bug fixes
  • Performance & cloud cost optimization
  • AI model retraining
  • Security updates & compliance monitoring
  • Feature additions & scaling

Most clients choose 12–24 month support retainers.

How do you ensure data privacy and security?
  • Zero-trust architecture by default
  • End-to-end encryption
  • ISO 27001-aligned processes
  • GDPR / CCPA / HIPAA-ready when required
  • Regular penetration testing & audits
  • Private AI deployments available
Can you integrate with our existing team or legacy systems?

Yes. Common scenarios include:

  • API & middleware integration with legacy systems
  • Modernizing monolithic applications
  • Extending existing codebases
  • Hybrid team collaboration
  • Knowledge transfer & documentation
What is your pricing / engagement model?
  • Fixed-price for clear MVPs & prototypes
  • Time & Materials for complex / evolving work
  • Dedicated team / extended capacity
  • AI product development retainer
